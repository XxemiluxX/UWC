English Club

This is one of the activities I've most enjoyed being a part of. Three years ago, with the help of Jo (an American voluntee of Cuerpo de Paz), we came up with the idea of ​​creating an English club.

It's a safe space where we practice our English through fun activities. Throughout the club's history, we've managed to carry out various activities so that students can improve their pronunciation and share enjoyable moments.

I am part of this project because I want to improve English at the school, and I feel that this was one of the best and most exciting ideas I've had to contribute educationally to the school.
